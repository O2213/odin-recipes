Jasmine Rice

Description

The best damn cooked rice youve ever had...at home.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Jasmine rice
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Measure the desired amount of rice (ex: 1 cup).
  2. Rinse the desired amount of rice.
  3. Measure 2x the amount of rice in water (ex: 1 cup of rice= 2 cups of water).
  4. Add the needed amount of water to a pot on a source of heat.
  5. Add a pinch of salt and a teaspoon of butter to water.
  6. Boil the water.
  7. Once boiling, add the rice to the water.
  8. Keep boiling and stir periodically over a period of 5 minutes.
  9. Turn heat to low and cover the rice with a lid for the pot for 10 minutes.
  10. After, let the rice dry away from heat source uncovered by lid for 10 minutes.
  11. Enjoy!
